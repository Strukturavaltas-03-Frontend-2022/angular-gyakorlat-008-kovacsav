The name of the event must have minimum 8, maximum 25 characters.
Date have to follow the english format: 'dd/mm/yyyy' - day/month/year - 1 or 2/2/4 characters.
Time have to follow the english format: 'hh:ss/am' or 'hh::ss/pm' - hour:second/am or pm.
The location of the event must have minimum 5, maximum 25 characters.
